Our Story

Built on 15 Years of Getting Travellers Where They Need to Go

GVS started in 2009 with a single office in Mumbai and a simple belief: applying for a visa shouldn't feel harder than planning the trip itself.

Today we process thousands of tourist visa applications every year across 150+ destinations — with a documentation-first process that keeps our rejection rate under 2%.

Our consultants track embassy requirements daily, so your application is always prepared against the latest rules — not last year's checklist.
